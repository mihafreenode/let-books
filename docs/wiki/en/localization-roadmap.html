Localization Roadmap

The localization roadmap defines how Let Books moves from English-first canonical content to progressively reviewed multilingual delivery.

Topics: Continuous Localization Localization Project Management

Stages

  1. English-first canonical content
  2. translation-ready metadata and structure
  3. AI draft generation where approved
  4. automated QA and spellcheck
  5. community and native-speaker review
  6. optional professional review for critical content

Priority order

  • public navigation and key docs
  • flagship blog and wiki pages
  • learning guides and accessibility text
  • diagrams, screenshots, and non-text assets
