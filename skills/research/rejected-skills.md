- Localization Governance

## Language Quality Checker

Why it looked like a skill:
- locale-aware rules and review value

Why it was rejected:

Absorbed by:
- Localization Governance

## Validator Engineering

Why it looked like a skill:
- the repository documents validators carefully and tests them directly

Why it was rejected as a top-level skill:
- the same principles govern tools, tests, scripts, workflows, and docs
- it behaves more like a governance pattern than an independent install target

Absorbed by:
- Engineering Governance

## Conversation Log Rendering
